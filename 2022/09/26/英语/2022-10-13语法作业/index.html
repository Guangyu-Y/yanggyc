语法作业20221013

Posted on 2022-09-26 Edited on 2022-12-27 In 英语

P15

  1. 找出谓语动词

  2. History written by winners contains bias. (谓语动词：contains)

  3. Consuming too much junk food negatively influences health.(谓语动词：influences)

  4. To punish a very young child is both wrong and foolish.(谓语动词：is)

  5. Attaining speeds of up to 60 miles per hour,cheetahs are perhaps the fastest of land animals.(谓语动词：are)

  6. Play date arranged by school provides children with the opportunity to socialise with friends.(谓语动词：provides)

  7. 判断该句是简单句

该句主谓结构：The pace has increased.

P133

  1. 简单句翻译

(1）近年来政界有更多的女性。

In recent years, there are more women in politices.
There are more females in politics in recent years

(2）网络让人们接触到几乎任何领域的信息。

The internet let pepole get information in almost any fields. [The internet enables people to get access to information in almost any fields.]

(3）将文化和旅游结合在一起是拯救文化的唯一办法。

Combing culture and tourism is the only way to save culture. [Combing culture with tourism is the only way to save culture.]

(4）在全球化的背景下，守传统让年轻人有种文化认同感。

In the age of global, following the tradition enables people have recognition of cultural identity.[In the age of globalization, following the tradition enables people to habe a sence of identity.]

(5）蔬菜的消耗量有所下降。

The consumption of vegetables has declined.[There is a decline in consumption of vegetables.]

(6）大城市里会有更多的交通工具的使用。

There are more use of transportation in big citys.

(7）人们利用动物是合理的。

Humans use animals is reasonable. [It is reasonable for people to use animals.]

(8）博物馆雇佣一些导游是有必要的。

Employing some guides is necessary for museums.[It is necessary for museums to employ some guides.]

(9）对老年工人的歧视将导致更严重的老龄化问题。

Discrimination to old workers will cause more serious ageing problems.[Discrimination against old workers will cause more serious ageing problems.]

(10）禁止在动物园饲养动物是很重要的。

It is important to ban feeding animals in zoos. [It is important to ban the practice of keeping animals in zoos.]

(11）参加团体运动可以增强体质。

Playing a team sport can improve health. [Participating in team sports can enhance physical fitness.]

(12）孩子们参加团体运动是很重要的。

It is important for children to play team sport. [It is important for children to get involved in team sports.]

(13）探索家庭背景在许多国家越来越受欢迎。

Exploring family backgrounds is more and more popular in many countries. [Exploring family background is becoming increasingly popular in many countries.]

(14）明天将有一场足球比赛。

There is a football match tomorrow. [There will be a football match tomorrow.]

(15）有一封从欧洲来的给你的信。

There is a letter from Europe for you.
